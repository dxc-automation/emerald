Larchmount Garden

Seamlessly blending
the outdoors with inside

Larchmount Garden

Landscape Design Build

We took an overgrown front yard that was nothing but mud and ice and carved out clean, paved pathways that lead to an entry stair creating a fluid aesthetic better serving everyday utility. A raised planter maintains the bee and butterfly-friendly garden and provides low maintenance gardening. Bench seating and an elongated staircase compliment the recently renovated entryway. Together, they help create a seamless relationship between interior and exterior and offer a popular informal social hub.
